About Us

Our Heritage

Our heritage is defined by a continuous commitment to biblical teaching and community service. Over years, we have empowered our members to live out their God-given potential through prayer, fellowship, and practical biblical teaching, shaping not just our congregation but the wider community around us.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

The Whole Church taking the Whole Gospel to the Whole World.

(KANISA LOTE, likieneza INJILI YOTE, ULIMWENGUNI KOTE)

Mission

The purpose of the Africa Gospel Church is to fulfill the Great Commandment and Great Commission of the Lord Jesus Christ according to the Great Plan.

Our Five Strategies

  • E1: Evangelizing the Unsaved
  • E2: Establishing Dynamic Churches
  • E3: Edifying Believers
  • E4: Equipping Leaders
  • E5: Exercising Compassion

Our Ministries

Children's Ministry

Nurturing faith in our youngest members through engaging lessons, activities, and loving care. Available during all Sunday services.

Youth Ministry

Empowering the next generation by providing a safe and exciting space for spiritual growth, where strong bonds are formed through mentorship and God's Word.

Women's Ministry

A community dedicated to equipping women to reach their full potential, emphasizing spiritual support, shared faith, and practical application of scripture.

Worship Team

Leading our congregation in heartfelt worship through music and song. New members always welcome to join!

Community Outreach

Spreading hope and the good news through dedicated outreach, helping neighbors find strength and inspiration in the Word.

Home Bible Study

Connect with others in intimate Bible study and fellowship groups that meet throughout the week in various locations.
